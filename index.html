About Me

Who I am & what I do

Hey, I'm Eric — a third-year Software Engineering student at UNSW based in Sydney, NSW. I love building things that are well-engineered, from clean REST APIs to low-level robotics systems.

I currently lead the Behaviours team for rUNSWift, UNSW's autonomous soccer robot team and five-time RoboCup World Champion. I design and implement decision-making systems that make humanoid robots play smart, competitive football.

When I'm not writing code, you'll find me on a golf course — I'm the President of the UNSW Social Golf Society and a two-time UniGames gold medallist. I'm also a Student Ambassador for UNSW Engineering, helping communicate the school's culture and vision.

I'm actively seeking software engineering internships for 2025–2026. I thrive in collaborative, fast-moving teams and care deeply about writing code that actually ships.
